Projects

  • Hodge number of complete intersections in product of projective spaces
    Sep 2022 – present

    First of all, I use the Hirzeburch-Riemann-Roch theorem to calculate the hodge number of the complete intersections in product of projective spaces. After that, I try to classify the complete intersetions in product of projective spaces by a restriction on hodge number--hodge-tate complete intersections. The case in single projective space is done by Pierre Deligne, I try to generalize this consequence, at least for the case when one of the projective spaces in the product is the projective line.
